Meet our Team

Buku-Buku Kafe is a restaurant-cafe in Parkway Corporate Center, Alabang specializing in Filipino and Southeast Asian comfort food. We focus on crafting great dining experiences and quality food delivery for our friends and family here in our hometown.


We are ready to serve you every day from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.
